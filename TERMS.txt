5. DATA HANDLING
  a) Audio data is captured from your microphone only when you explicitly
     activate recording (via hotkey or voice trigger).
  b) Audio is sent to the STT provider you have configured for transcription.
  c) Transcripts are stored locally in a SQLite database on your machine.
  d) Knowledge vault notes are stored as local .md files in the location you
     specify during setup.
  e) No data is sent to the Software developers or any party other than
     your configured service providers.

6. VOICE ENROLLMENT

representations of your voice) are stored locally in your Memory Vault. These
are not transmitted to any remote service.
